Sayantan Choudhury

I am a 4th year PhD candidate in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University. I am very fortunate to work under the supervision of Nicolas Loizou. My research is in the area of Optimization and Machine Learning. My work analyses Extragradient Methods for solving structured non-convex problems and Federated Learning. I am also interested in adaptive methods and methods for solving large-scale linear equations.

Before coming to JHU, I completed my B.Stat and M.Stat from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. During my Bachelors and Masters, I was involved in several research projects at the Indian Statistical Institute, India, and the Centre for Science of Student Learning. After joining JHU, I completed my MSE in Applied Mathematics and Statistics with a concentration on Optimization.
